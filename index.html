On your own

Pick a schema & stick with it!

Schema is just a fancy word for how your database is set up (table names, field names, unique keys, domains).

Don't start anything until you've picked your schema and level of detail.

But be willing to adjust your schema over time (changing needs, lessons learned).

Data Collection

3 Methods:

  • digitizing
  • edit existing data
  • field survey

For any method, you'll need to know what things look like.

For location aerial imagery works great!

For obstructions and crosswalk accessibility, you'll need a street level view.

Mapillary

Collect & upload street level imagery (or even sidewalk imagery!).

Mapillary

View it in OpenStreetMap and link from QGIS (go2mapillary plugin).

OpenStreetCam

Probably best off using Mapillary, but this is another option viewable in OpenStreetMap.

Or take your own geotagged photos and import into QGIS.

Search for Import Geotagged Pictures and point it to the images folder. You will save the result to your geopackage as a Photos layer.

To quickly view the images and highlight the associated point, go to Database, eVis, eVis Event Browser.
